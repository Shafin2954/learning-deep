# Reinforcement Learning

Course 13 of 20. Tabular to deep RL, Sutton-grounded — value functions, policy gradients, and PPO.

## Chapters
1. The RL problem: agent-environment, reward
2. Multi-armed bandits: explore vs exploit, UCB
3. Markov Decision Processes and value functions
4. Dynamic programming: policy and value iteration
5. Monte Carlo prediction and control
6. Temporal-difference learning: SARSA and Q-learning
7. n-step methods and eligibility traces
8. Function approximation and the deadly triad
9. Deep Q-Networks: replay and target nets
10. Policy gradients: REINFORCE and baselines
11. Actor-critic: A2C and PPO
12. Continuous control: DDPG, TD3, SAC
13. Model-based RL and planning
14. Capstone: Tabular to deep RL from scratch
